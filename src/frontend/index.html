About

This application fetches weather data and calculates a rideability score based on current conditions. It is designed to help motorcyclists decide whether it is safe to ride.

Rideability Score Calculation Logic

The rideability score is calculated based on the following criteria:

  • Precipitation: Subtracts 3 points, Snow subtracts 8 points
  • Visibility: Less than 2 km subtracts 5 points, between 2 km and 5 km subtracts 2 points, and between 5 km and 10 km subtracts 1 point
  • Temperature: Below 0°C subtracts 2 points, above 30°C subtracts 2 points, between 5°C and 25°C subtracts 1 point
  • Wind: Above 5 m/s subtracts 1 point, above 10 m/s subtracts 2 points
